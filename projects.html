Stakeholder Engagement Assessment Matrix (SEAM)

Using PMI's framework, the SEAM evaluates stakeholders' current engagement level ("C") against desired engagement ("D"). Engagement varies from unaware to leading, influencing the approach: keeping satisfied, managing closely, monitoring, or keeping informed. Concerns and issues guide this assessment, highlighting areas for improvement. Some Stakeholders require specific approaches to address their needs, ensuring project success. Regular monitoring helps adapt strategies to maintain positive engagement and address concerns promptly
